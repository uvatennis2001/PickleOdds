DUPR MODEL
Team DUPR = average of both players. Win prob uses logistic function: 1/(1+10^((oppDUPR−myDUPR)×k)) with k=1.8 calibrated for pickleball scoring variance.
TOURNAMENT ODDS
Expected wins = sum of win probs vs each opponent. Tournament win odds reflect probability of finishing 1st in round robin standings.
MONEYLINE FORMAT
Negative = favorite (bet that to win $100). Positive = underdog (win that on $100 bet). Even = +/−100 pick'em.
SCORING NOTE
Side-out scoring to 15 increases variance vs rally scoring. Upsets are more likely. Odds reflect true probability without house edge (no vig).
